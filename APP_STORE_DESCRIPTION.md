# Chat42 - Local AI Assistant

Chat42 is a powerful, privacy-focused AI chat interface that lets you interact with local and cloud-based large language models on your Mac. Run AI models locally or connect to cloud services with a single, intuitive interface.

## Key Features

• **Multiple AI Backends**: Support for Ollama, MLX (Apple Silicon), and Gateway (OpenAI-compatible APIs)
• **Privacy First**: All conversations stay on your device - no data is sent to external servers
• **Seamless Switching**: Easily switch between different AI models and services
• **Conversation History**: Persistent chat history with automatic title generation
• **Customizable Settings**: Adjust system prompts, temperature, and other parameters
• **Dark/Light Mode**: Adaptive interface that matches your system preferences
• **Model Management**: Download, load, and manage local AI models directly from the app

## For Local AI (Ollama)

Connect to your local Ollama instance to run models directly on your Mac. Perfect for privacy-conscious users who want complete control over their AI processing.

## For Apple Silicon (MLX)

Take advantage of Apple's optimized MLX framework for fast, efficient inference on M1/M2/M3/M4/M5 Macs. Download and run models from Hugging Face directly within the app.

## For Cloud AI (Gateway)

Connect to OpenAI-compatible APIs including OpenAI, LiteLLM, and other services. Configure API keys and access models from popular AI providers.

## Secure & Private

Chat42 prioritizes your privacy:
- All conversations are stored locally on your device
- No personal data is collected or transmitted
- Network connections are secured with proper certificates
- No analytics or tracking is performed

## Requirements

- macOS 13.0 or later
- For MLX support: Apple Silicon Mac (M1/M2/M3/M4/M5)

## Privacy Notice

Chat42 does not collect or transmit any personal data. All conversations and model usage occur locally on your device. When connecting to external services, data is transmitted according to the privacy policies of those services.
