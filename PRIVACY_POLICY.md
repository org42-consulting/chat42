# Privacy Policy for Chat42

## Information We Collect

Chat42 does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data. The application is designed to be completely private and secure:

- All conversations remain on your device
- No user data is sent to external servers
- No analytics or usage tracking is performed
- No personal information is collected or stored

## Data Usage

The application only accesses:
- Local files for conversation history persistence
- Network connections to external AI services (Ollama, API endpoints) when configured by the user
- System resources for application operation

## External Services

When connecting to external AI services:
- Chat42 forwards your prompts and requests to those services
- The service provider's privacy policy applies to how they handle your data
- Chat42 does not collect or transmit any additional information beyond what's required for the AI interaction

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- Local storage encryption (when available on your system)
- Secure network communication with proper certificates
- No data is stored on our servers

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will be effective immediately after posting.
